2022/2023 Registration Guidelines

Guidelines for Registration for Fresh and Returning Students

a. (Fresh Students Only) Complete Fresh Student Biodata, Medical, Sport and Other Details form: it will require you uploading the scanned copy (colored) of the following documents

  • O'Level Certificate/Statement of result
  • Birth Certificate/Sworn Affidavit
  • Attestation Letter
  • Signed Declaration of Good Conduct (download copy here)
  • UTME Admission Letter
  • UTME Result
  • ND/NCE/A'Level Certificate or Statement of Result (DE Only)

b. Check your email for Student portal username & Password for login

c. Retrieve your FUNAAB email account to grant you access to G Suite for Education.

d. Login to Generate school fee invoice

e. Pay invoice online or via other channels

f. Generate School Fee Receipt

g. Register for Courses

h. Pay Student Union Dues

i. Bidding for Hostel to be announced later.

j. (Fresh Students Only) Login to upload Signed Matriculation Oath

Deadline for Payment of School Fees and Course Registration!!!

Wednessday 2nd Monday 28th May, 2023 (12pm).
